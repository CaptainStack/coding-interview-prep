Full Stack Developer

I am a full stack developer who has been programming over 6 years. I earned my BS in informatics with a specialization in human-computer interaction from the University of Washington.

I specialize in web development and have worked in NodeJS, Ruby on Rails, and Drupal. During my time as a professional web developer I have worked at education non-profit Code.org and sports software company Fencing Time.

Journalist

I am a writer for The International Examiner and have been a journalist since 2008 when I began working for my high school paper The Nordic News.

In college I worked at The Daily of the University of Washington where I focused on arts writing and film critique. I was editor of the Arts & Leisure section for a year and eventually worked as Editor in Chief.

I also infrequently write on a wide varity of topics including politics, technology, and art on a Medium.com blog.
